The Case for Change

Why Anything? Why Glean? Why Now?

🔴 Why Anything?

  • CS reacts to issues already visible elsewhere in Slack, Gong, or Zendesk — but no one connects the dots
  • Every team operates on different slices of truth across Salesforce, Jira, Slack, Gong, Confluence, and Zendesk
  • Tribal knowledge is trapped in Slack threads — and Slack AI was removed
  • Ramp time for new CSEs is 8-10 weeks because info is siloed and knowledge is linear
  • Procore has 6+ AI tools (Rovo, Scout, Gemini, GongAI, ChatGPT, Claude) and none of them talk to each other

🔵 Why Glean?

  • Only platform that indexes all 15+ systems into one Enterprise Knowledge Graph and reasons across them in a single query
  • 120+ pre-built connectors — Salesforce, Slack, Jira, Confluence, Gong, Google, Zendesk, and more
  • Model-agnostic: use Claude, Gemini, or GPT — best model for each task, not locked to one vendor
  • Permission-aware by design — every answer respects source-system access controls
  • No-code agent builder: any CSE can build workflows in minutes, not months

🟢 Why Now?

  • Executive initiative to consolidate internal tools and finalize AI platform strategy
  • Google bundling AgentSpace into workspace renewal at a net wash — the window to choose a real platform is closing
  • Moveworks acquisition by ServiceNow creates roadmap uncertainty
  • Each department is independently buying AI tools — governance gap is growing
  • Scout (Workato) is 3+ months delayed and limited to point-and-click GTM workflows

Top Glean Agent Use Cases for Procore CS

Customer Health & Churn Risk Detection

Salesforce → Gong → Slack → Zendesk → Usage Data

Agent monitors sentiment signals across support tickets, Gong calls, Slack mentions, and product usage. Surfaces at-risk accounts weeks before renewal with a confidence score and recommended playbook.

↳ Proactive churn prevention, protects GRR — not reactive firefighting

Pre-Sales → Post-Sales Handoff Agent

Salesforce → Gong → Slack → Google Drive

Auto-generates a concise handoff document with full deal context, stakeholders, technical requirements, risks, and next steps for CSE/CET teams. Zero-loss handoffs; post-sales teams start Day 1 with complete context.

↳ Eliminates "who said what?" and reduces onboarding friction by 50%+

QBR Prep & Account 360 Agent

Salesforce → Gong → Jira → Zendesk → Confluence → Slack

One-click generation of a complete account snapshot: open tickets, feature requests, recent call sentiment, NPS trends, expansion signals, and usage patterns — pulled from 6+ systems in seconds, not hours.

↳ Customer.io hypothesis: double QBRs/month = 4 new expansion opps monthly

Deployment Runbook Generator

Confluence → Google Drive → Jira

Auto-generates a step-by-step deployment runbook customized to each customer's tech stack and connected data sources. Standardizes every deployment using prior implementation records and best practices.

↳ Reduce implementation timelines by 30%+ and eliminate tribal knowledge dependency

Product FAQ Buddy Bot (Embedded in Slack)

Confluence → Highspot → Google Drive → Slack → Jira

Instant answers to any product, pricing, or technical question — right from the browser extension or Slack. Zero SME dependency. Today, CSEs swivel-chair between Highspot, Confluence, and Slack. Glean is the one-stop shop.

↳ Answer technical questions live on customer calls — zero wait time for SME input

Cross-Project Lessons Learned

Confluence → Google Drive → Jira → Slack

Search across all past project docs, retrospectives, and delivery notes to find patterns and best practices. Scale institutional knowledge and avoid repeating mistakes on every new customer engagement.

↳ New CSEs ramp 30-50% faster by accessing collective organizational wisdom instantly
Path Forward

High-Level Pilot → Rollout Plan

Weeks 1-2 · Foundation

Connect & Configure

Connect core systems (Slack, Salesforce, Gong, Jira, Confluence, Google Workspace, Zendesk). Glean auto-indexes and builds the enterprise knowledge graph with permissions from Day 1. No engineering required — fully turnkey connector setup.

Weeks 2-4 · Prove Value

Pilot with CSE Power Users

Select 20-30 CSEs as pilot users. Deploy 2-3 hero agents (Account 360, QBR Prep, Product FAQ Bot). Measure time saved per QBR prep, ticket investigation time, and self-service answer rate. Establish success criteria and baseline metrics.

Weeks 4-8 · Expand

Build & Scale Agent Library

CSE champions build additional agents (Churn Risk, Handoff Agent, Lessons Learned). Run enablement workshops — any CSE can build an agent in the no-code builder. Expand to SolSpec and SE teams with their own use cases. Track adoption, satisfaction, and business metrics.

Weeks 8-12 · Enterprise Decision

Executive Readout & Full Rollout

Present ROI data: time saved, ticket metrics, adoption rates, tool consolidation savings ($700K+ from replacing Slack AI + Moveworks alone). Make the case for enterprise-wide deployment as Procore's unified Work AI platform — one governed tool replacing 6+ fragmented AI experiments.
